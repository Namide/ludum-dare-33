#Denoky

Denoky is a mix between a SHMUP and a 3D platform game.


Rules

Crushes the pawns and avoids the bullets


Controls

Moves:  Arrows
Jump:  Spacebar
Mute music:  M
Mute sounds:  S


Details

* Language: Haxe 3
* FlashPlayer: >11.8
* Samples mades with LabChirp and music with autotracker
* Libs: Heaps and Actuate
